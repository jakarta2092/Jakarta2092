TL;DR Jakarta 2092

A gritty cyberpunk thriller set in Jakarta, Indonesia.

About the Book

Jakarta 2092, a city scarred by floods and decay, abandoned by the government and most of its people. Only those with nowhere else to go remained.

Now the streets are ruled by debt syndicates and thugs.

In the middle of it all, former detective Raka comes across the aftermath of a young woman’s suicide — and something inside him snaps. He decides it’s time for the city to rise again, to thrive, and to be rid of the predators who keep it chained.

With the help of a hacker, a hired muscle, and the stolen echo of faith carried through prayer implants, he gathers a crew and together they ignite a revolution.

TL;DR Jakarta 2092 is a gritty, fast-paced dystopian cyberpunk thriller set in Jakarta, Indonesia — a story about survival, trust, religion as power, and the thin line between guidance and control.
